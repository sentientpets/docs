### Trick Training System

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Trick">
    Choose from available tricks in the training menu. New tricks unlock as your relationship strengthens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Training Sessions">
    Conduct daily training sessions using specific interaction patterns. Each trick requires 5-7 days of consistent training.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reinforcement">
    Once learned, tricks require occasional reinforcement to maintain proficiency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Performance">
    Trained tricks can be performed on command, providing happiness boosts and special animations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Available Tricks

| Trick | Training Period | Benefits | Special Effect |
|-------|-----------------|----------|----------------|
| High Five | 5 days | +10% Happiness | Small token reward bonus |
| Spin | 5 days | +2% all stats | Brief animation sequence |
| Play Dead | 6 days | +15% Happiness | Humorous animation sequence |
| Fetch | 7 days | +5% Energy, +10% Happiness | Interactive mini-game |
| Dance | 7 days | +20% Happiness | Extended animation sequence |

<Note>
Each pet develops aptitudes for different tricks based on their personality. Pay attention to which training activities your pet responds to most enthusiastically.
</Note>
