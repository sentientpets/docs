## Personality Shaping

Beyond basic care, you can actively shape your pet's personality through focused interactions:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Trait Reinforcement">
    Encourage specific personality traits by consistently responding to and rewarding certain behaviors:
    
    - **For Playfulness:** Prioritize play activities and respond quickly to play requests
    - **For Independence:** Allow minor hunger/happiness fluctuations without immediate intervention
    - **For Affection:** Provide immediate care when notifications appear and frequent check-ins
    - **For Curiosity:** Regularly introduce new activities and experiences
    
    Reinforcement requires 2-3 weeks of consistent patterns to create lasting trait changes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Behavior Modification">
    You can modify unwanted behaviors through consistent response patterns:
    
    - **Excessive Neediness:** Gradually extend time between interactions while maintaining good stats
    - **Low Energy:** Regular high-activity play sessions to build stamina
    - **Picky Eating:** Rotate food options while reinforcing positive responses
    - **Anxiety:** Develop extremely consistent care schedules to build trust
    
    Behavior modification usually takes 10-14 days to show results, with full changes requiring 3-4 weeks.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
