## Token Release Schedule

$PURR tokens are released gradually to maintain a healthy ecosystem and prevent inflation:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/token-release.png" alt="PURR Token Release Schedule" />
</Frame>

### Community Rewards Emission

The primary distribution of $PURR comes through continuous community rewards, released according to this schedule:

- **Year 1:** 250,000,000 tokens (50% of rewards allocation)
- **Year 2:** 125,000,000 tokens (25% of rewards allocation)
- **Year 3:** 75,000,000 tokens (15% of rewards allocation)
- **Year 4:** 50,000,000 tokens (10% of rewards allocation)

This deflationary emission model ensures that early adopters receive higher rewards while maintaining long-term sustainability.
